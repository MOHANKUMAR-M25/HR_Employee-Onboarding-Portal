# IT Access Guide

> Mock policy document for the RAG pipeline. Retrieved by the agent when
> deciding what equipment / access a hire should get.

## §1 Contractor access

Contractors use **BYOD** (bring-your-own-device) with mandatory MDM enrolment
and a **scoped VPN** profile limited to the systems named in their SOW. No asset
purchase order is raised and no managed laptop is shipped.

## §2 FTE access

Full-time employees receive a **managed laptop** provisioned to the regional IT
standard, full SSO, and standard application entitlements for their department.
