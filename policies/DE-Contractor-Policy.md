# DE Contractor Onboarding Policy

> Mock policy document. This stands in for one of the PDFs a real RAG pipeline
> would index. The ADLC agent's `policy_rag` tool "retrieves" sections from here
> when a hire is a **contractor based in the EU/Germany**.

## §2 Engagement

Contractors are engaged via a statement of work (SOW). No FTE benefits, payroll,
or equity are provisioned. A contractor liaison is assigned in place of the FTE
buddy program.

## §4 Data & Compliance

Per **GDPR Art. 13**, a data-processing notice must be acknowledged before any
system access is granted. The works council (**Betriebsrat**) must be notified
of the engagement before the start date.

## §5 Equipment

Contractors use BYOD with MDM enrolment and a scoped VPN profile. No asset
purchase order is raised.
