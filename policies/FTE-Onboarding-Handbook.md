# FTE Onboarding Handbook

> Mock policy document for the RAG pipeline. Retrieved by the agent for
> full-time hires.

## §3 First week

Full-time hires are:

- enrolled in regional **payroll & benefits**,
- paired with an onboarding **buddy** (see Buddy Program Guide),
- issued a **managed laptop** per regional IT standards, and
- enrolled in the live **new-hire cohort** orientation (or self-paced
  orientation + a manager 1:1 if they join mid-quarter).
